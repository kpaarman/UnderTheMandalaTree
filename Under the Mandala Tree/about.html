Pregnancy Yoga/Massage Centre For you and your baby

Instructor

Linda Norman

I am a Teacher with 3 'babies' of my own, ranging in ages from 4 to 22! I'm a primary school teacher who retrained so I could fit my career around my youngest daughter- who you will get to meet if you join one of the pregnancy yoga classes. Laila’s the VIP team member, in charge of handing out belts and blocks – colour co-ordinated of course! I teach baby massage, yoga, pregnancy yoga, Birth Preparation Workshops and also Hypnobirthing. I believe that Dads also have a very important role during labour and also after birth so I also offer the popular Baby Yoga with Daddy courses as well as Birth Preparation Classes. I love Bournemouth and believe in the importance of giving back to my community and this I do in part through volunteering as the Christchurch Netmums Chairmum. Mamababybliss Bournemouth also supports a local charity, The Amelia Grace Rainbow Fund.
